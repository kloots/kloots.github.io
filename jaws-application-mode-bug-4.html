JAWS ARIA application mode example #2

Issue Summary

When moving focus into an ARIA application context JAWS doesn't play a sound to indicate the switch into to forms mode.

Repro Steps

  1. Press F6 to move focus to the first ARIA listitem instance on this page.
  2. Notice JAWS does not play a sound to indicate forms is enabled even though form mode has been enabled.
